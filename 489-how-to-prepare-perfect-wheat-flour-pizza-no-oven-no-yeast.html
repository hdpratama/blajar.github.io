Wheat flour pizza no oven no yeast instructions

  1. Assemble everything together..
  2. In a mixing bowl add wheat flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and oil. Mix it well. Now gradually add curd and knead a soft dough. Cover it with a cloth. Keep it aside to rest for 20 minutes..
  3. Take a wok. Spread salt in, put a stand and place a plate over it. Cover with lid. Preheat it for 10 minutes..
  4. After 20 minutes. Grease you hands, press and knead dough to soft..
  5. Divide it in 4-5 equal parts. Take one portion, roll into round shape Roti. With fork prick the Roti as shown in picture..
  6. Place it in a greased plate in preheated wok. Cover it, bake it for 8-10 minutes on medium heat. Prepare all the roti (pizza base) by this method..
  7. Take a prepared pizza base. Spread butter. After that apply some pizza sauce..
  8. Spread onions, tomatoes, capsicum and sweet corn..
  9. At last cover it with grated cheese. Sprinkle some salt..
  10. Heat a heavy based tawa. Place this pizza on it. Cover it and bake on low heat until cheese melts. Check the base it should not be burn..
  11. Transfer it in a serving plate. Grate some more cheese. Sprinkle oregano and chilli flakes. Serve it hot..
