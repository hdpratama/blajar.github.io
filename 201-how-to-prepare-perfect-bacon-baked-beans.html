Ingredients of Bacon Baked Beans

  1. It's 4-6 strips of cooked bacon (save the grease!).
  2. It's 1 tbsp of bacon grease.
  3. It's 28-32 oz of B&M baked beans (if you can’t find them, just stay away from really soft beans like Bush).
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of ketchup.
  5. It's 1/4 cup of dark brown sugar.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of dry mustard seed.
  7. It's 1/2 tsp of Cayenne pepper (even better - pepper flakes, if you can find them).
  8. It's of Optional: 1/2 onion sliced and fried in bacon grease until light brown.
