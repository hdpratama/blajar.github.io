Baked Cauliflower instructions

  1. Wash then soak cauliflower in a salt water for 30 mins. Then wash it again.
  2. In a deep pot, put cauliflower then add water until it will submerged. Add pound garlic, sesame oil, black pepper, chicken broth. Then boil it for 20 mins or until half cook..
  3. When done, remove and throw the water. Place in a plate and sprinkle paprika and grated cheese..
  4. Preheat oven at 250c and bake it for 10 mins. Served.
