No Oven, No Yeast Veg Pizza recipe step by step

  1. Mix self rising flour and greek yogurt.
  2. Knead it for 5 minutes and make a dough out of it.
  3. Take half portion of the dough and flatten it in a pizza pan.
  4. Pinch the sides to give it shape.
  5. Add some pizza sauce and healthy toppings like capsicum, onion, tomato, corn.
  6. Add mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Brush oil on the sides.
  8. Preheat a kadai for 10 minutes on high flame.
  9. Place the pizza pan inside the kadai and bake it for 10-12 minutes on high heat.
  10. Sprinkle some oregano and chilli flakes to spice it up.
  11. Slice it and enjoy!.
