No oven cheese pizza with wheat flour no yeast || pan pizza step by step

  1. Take 1 cup wheat flour, add 1/2 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp sugar, 1 tsp baking powder and 1/2 tsp soda.
  2. Now take the curd and knead the dough If required add water.
  3. Cover it with a wet cotton towel and keep aside. Till that time we will make the masala ready.
  4. Grate the cheese, cut capsicum onion and paneer in cubes. In a pan add little oil and sauteed this vegetable as we are making in pan we have to stir fry it.
  5. Add pinch of salt oregano and red chili flakes. Dont over cook.
  6. Now the dough is ready we will knead it and make a big ball out of it. Then roll it like a roti but it should be little thick.
  7. After that pork it with fork and put it into the pan..
  8. Cover the pan and let it cook for 4 to5 min.
  9. One side we will fry.
  10. Now our base is ready.
  11. Turn the base towards the cook part and add pizza sauce to it. Then the vegetable and put it into the flame.
  12. Flame should be low. And cook it for 4 to 5 minutes.
  13. Now take out the pizza it's ready to serve.
  14. For instance pizza sauce in a bowl add tomato sauce, 1 tsp oregano, 1 tsp chili flakes, 1/4th black pepper powder and 1/4 th black salt..
