Homemade pan pizza(no oven) instructions

  1. Mix your flour,yeast and salt together.
  2. Add your tbsp of sugar and 1/2 cup of warm water and mix together,then add your 2tbsp of olive oil and mix together.
  3. Need to form a dough,roll it in a circle form and use a fork to chock it,then cover with a plastic wrap.
  4. Close with a the pan cover and leave to rise for 20mis.
  5. To make your sauce,blend your tomatoes and place in a pan,cook for 20mins then add your garlic,oil and Maggie,then finally add your chili pepper and leave to cool.
  6. Cut your sausage into round shapes and cut your green pepper,cucumber,red pepper into cubes and then grate your president cheese.
  7. When your dough is ready,apply your sauce,then add your grated cheese.
  8. Add your cucumber,add your red pepper and then add you sausage.
  9. Add your pineapple,add your cheese again and then place the pan on a stove.
  10. Close with a nonstick cover and bake in a low heat until your cheese is melted.and then finally your pan pizza is ready to serve.
