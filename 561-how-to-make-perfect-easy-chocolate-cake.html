Easy chocolate cake step by step

  1. At first in a grinder add 2pkt bourbon biscuits and make fine powder. Now in a mixing bowl add the biscuits powder and milk,mixed well..
  2. Now add 1tsp baking powder and 1tsp milk, mixed well and make the cake batter. Now grease the cake tin with refined oil. Pour the batter in the cake tin..
  3. Tap it and bake for 40-45 mints in low-medium flame. For making easy chocolate sauce,in a bowl add 1tbsp sugar and 1tbsp butter,mixed well. Then add 1tbsp coco powder and 2tbsp hot milk,mixed well until it's make creamy..
  4. Now check it,cake is done. Gurnish with chocolate sauce and some colourful sprinkles..
  5. Most easy and super tasty Chocolate cake is ready to served..
