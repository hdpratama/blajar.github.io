Ingredients of Lemon cake without butter and no oven

  1. It's 100 g of sugar.
  2. Prepare 300 g of flour.
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder.
  4. Prepare 3 of eggs.
  5. It's 4 of table spoons veg oil.
  6. You need of Grated lemon and juiced lemon.
  7. You need of Warm water or milk.

Lemon cake without butter and no oven step by step

  1. Mix your sugar with vegetable oil.
  2. In a different bowl, beat your eggs till foamy.
  3. Add eggs to the sugar and stir evenly.
  4. Add the lemon juice and grated rind and stir..
  5. Sift in flour and baking powder into the mixture then stir.
  6. Add milk or warm water bit by bit till you get your favorable flow.
  7. Prepare your baking tin.
  8. Prepare a sufuria and put in sand.. Cover and preheat for 5 minutes under medium heat.
  9. Put your mixture in your tin and bake for one hour under medium heat.
  10. It's ready when you put a knife in and comes out clear..
