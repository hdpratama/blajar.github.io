Ingredients of No oven Decadent Chocolate Cake

  1. Prepare 2 cup of whole wheat flour(you can also use All-purpose flour instead of Wheat flour).
  2. You need 1 cup of milk.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of malai/cream.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of baking powder.
  5. You need 1 tsp of baking soda.
  6. You need 3 tbsp of cocoa powder.

No oven Decadent Chocolate Cake step by step

  1. Blend Milk,Cream/Malai and sugar till sugar is well dissolved (2 mins).The liquid mixture is ready..
  2. Sieve wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda & cocoa powder in a bowl.Add the liquid mixture and mix it well. Dust the Cake pot with ghee & flour so the cake doesnt stick to it.
  3. We will cook it in Cake pot on Stove on low flame for 1 hour.To check if the bake is well baked poke the cake with a knife,cake mixture shouldnt stick to the knife..
