Ingredients of Jowar Bread

  1. It's 175 gm of jowar flour.
  2. It's 75 gm of Wheat flour or maida.
  3. It's 2 tsp of dry yeast.
  4. Prepare 2 tsp of sugar.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of butter.
  6. You need 2 tsp of + 1 tsp oil.
  7. It's of Mixed herbs,sesame seeds,chilli flakes for toppings.
  8. It's of Water.
  9. It's of Salt.

Jowar Roti (Sorghum Flat Bread) is delicious and healthy. It is traditional flat bread best made without using a rolling-pin. Jowar Roti is a staple of Western and Southern Indian states. It is best made by hand (without using a rolling-pin) but if you can't go ahead and use a rolling-pin.

Jowar Bread instructions

  1. Mix jowar flour,wheat flour n salt.
  2. Mix yeast n sugar in warm water n keep it aside to get it activate..
  3. Mix yeast mixture into jowar flour n with the help of water,knead the dough for 5 to 7 minutes. The texture will be different than Maida bread..
  4. Keep this dough for the first proofing (for 30 minutes or more).
  5. After first proofing,punch it,then put in greased baking containers or dish with your desired shape for 20 to 25 minutes.
  6. In preheat oven,bake these jowar breads at 180 ' for 20 to 25 minutes. Apply butter as soon as it gets baked.n cover it with thick towel for 30 minutes..
