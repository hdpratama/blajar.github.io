Fried rice step by step

  1. In a vessel add water, soya sauce, green chilli, Maggi cube bring to a boil.
  2. Now add rice cook till rice cooked.
  3. Diced veggies.
  4. In a pan add garlic, onion, French beans, capsicum & saute well.
  5. Add salt & all the sauce stir well.
  6. Lastly add rice & spring onion mix well. Enjoy wth potato chilli or any gravy.
