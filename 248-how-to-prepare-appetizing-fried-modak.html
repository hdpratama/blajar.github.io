Ingredients of Fried Modak

  1. You need 2 bowls of wheat flour.
  2. It's 1 1/2 bowl of grated coconut.
  3. Prepare 1 bowl of Jaggery.
  4. You need To taste of Cardomom powder.
  5. You need As needed of Oil for frying.
  6. It's Pinch of salt.
  7. It's As needed of Milk and water.

Fried Modak step by step

  1. In one pan add coconut and Jaggery mix well and cook till Jaggery completely..
  2. Mix with coconut. Add cardomom powder..
  3. Let it cool.
  4. Now take wheat flour add gradually mixture of water and milk 1:1 proportion. Make medium soft dough.
  5. Roll medium thick chapati and with cookie cutter make small round shaped puris.
  6. Now stuff above coconut mixture in each puri and prepare Madaks..
