Ingredients of Air fried shrimp

  1. You need Teaspoon of minced garlic.
  2. You need of Extra large shrimp.
  3. You need of Kosher salt.
  4. You need 2 teaspoons of butter.

Get ready because dinner is about to be served! Serve these Shrimp on a bed of Perfect Instant Pot Rice, or with Easy Fried Rice and some Vegetable Stir Fry for a splendid dinner. Recipe Notes and Tips Wait until the halfway mark to spray your shrimp. Add shrimp into air fryer basket in one layer.

Air fried shrimp step by step

  1. Thaw and peel shrimp, sprinkle with kosher salt..
  2. Melt butter in microwave and mix with garlic..
  3. Mix shrimp with butter and garlic..
  4. Preheat air fryer to 400°. Place shrimp, single layer in fryer basket..
  5. Cook for 3 minutes, remove and turn. Cook additional 1 minute..
