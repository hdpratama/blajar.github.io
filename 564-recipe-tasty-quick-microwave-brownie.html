Quick Microwave Brownie step by step

  2. Now take milk in a small cup add instant coffee in it. Mix it well and add in chocolate mixture..
  3. Further add all the dry ingredients in the wet chocolate mixture..
  4. Pour the batter in moulds and microwave it for 1 min. 30 sec..
  5. After cool down Brownie is ready to serve with icecream n some chocochips..
