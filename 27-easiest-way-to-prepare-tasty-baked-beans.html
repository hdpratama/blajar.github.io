Ingredients of Baked beans

  1. You need 1 lb of rinsed white navy beans or pinto beans.
  2. It's 1 of medium onion peeled.
  3. Prepare 1/2 lb of bacon or salt pork.
  4. You need 1 1/2 tsp of salt.
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp of dry mustard.
  6. You need 1/8 tsp of pepper.
  7. Prepare 3 tbls of brown sugar.
  8. Prepare 1/3 cup of molasses.
  9. Prepare 1/2 of ketchup.
  10. You need 1 tbls of yellow mustard.
  11. Prepare 2 tsp of cider vinegar.

Baked beans instructions

  1. Soak the washed beans in enough cold water to cover overnight.
  2. In the morning add water to cover the beans if needed and simmer till beans are tender 1 hour maybe more..
  3. Meanwhile put molasses ketchup, vinegar, mustard in a bowl and mix. Add spices and brown sugar and 1 cup of boiling water and mix well until smooth.
  4. Place onion in deep casserole dish or bean crock, (I use a 3 liter cast iron pot). Add the bacon or salt pork and spoon the beans and the liquid they boiled in over the onion and pork. Add the molasses mixture gently to the beans.
  5. Place in a 300 ° oven for 5 1/2 hours. Check once in a while to make sure they are still covered in a bit of liquid..
