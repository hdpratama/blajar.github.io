Ingredients of Baked Sakarpara

  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of wheat flour.
  2. You need 1 cup of milk.
  3. Prepare to taste of Jaggery.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of cardamom powder.
  5. It's 2 tsp of ghee.

Baked Sakarpara step by step

  1. First heat milk and add jaggery to it..
  2. As jaggery melts milk may start curdling..
  3. Switch off the flame and let it cool..
  4. In a bowl add wheat flour cardamom powder and knead the dough with jaggery syrup..
  5. Add 1tsp ghee and knead again smoothly..
  6. Let the dough rest for 10mins..
  7. After 10mins divide the dough into two parts.
  8. Make a round shape and roll with a rolling pin.
  9. Cut with a knife-diamond shape.
  10. Put them in a greased baking tray.
  11. Bake them in oven @200 for 12-15mins.
  12. Ready...enjoy..
  13. You can fry them too in hot oil on medium flame *.
