Friedrice instructions

  1. Dice your carrots, peas and carrots, get ready your other ingredients..
  2. Wash your rice with salt till its void of starch..
  3. Place a pot on fire, add vegetable oil, then pour in the washed rice..
  4. Fry with half of your spices, including the friedrice spice, stir continuously until its perfectly fried.
  5. Add in your meat stock and water. Gradually add in your water, till the rice is done..
  6. Set up another pot on fire, add in your margarine, allow it to melt, then add in a little amount of oil..
  7. Add in your chopped pepper and diced liver, leave for 1 minutes, then add in the carrot, peas, sweetcorn. & leave for 3 mins..
  8. Stir continuously for 3 minutes, after which you turn off the heat.
  9. Add in your fried vegetables into the rice, stir until both are entirely mixed.
  10. Cover for 2mins after which you turn off the heat..
