Ingredients of Vanilla cake no oven

  1. It's 1 cup of maida / all-purpose flour.
  2. You need 3/4 cup of powdered sugar.
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup of milk.
  4. You need 1/2 cup of fresh malai/cream.
  5. You need 1 tsp of baking powder.
  6. You need 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  7. It's 2 drops of vanilla essence.
  8. Prepare as needed of Whipping cream.
  9. You need as needed of Food color.

Vanilla cake no oven step by step

  1. Beat the cream/ malai and sugar.
  2. Seive all dry ingredients and mix to above.
  3. Now add milk beat with electric beater.
  4. Grease tin pour batter.
  5. Preheat a aluminium pan,place stand in it,place this tin on it.
  6. Cover the aluminium pan.
  7. Don't add sand or salt in the aluminium pan.
  8. Bake it for 20 minutes on low flame.
  9. Base is ready let it cool down.
  10. Garnish it with whipping cream,beating the cream with electric beater add color and essence to cream of your choice.
