Ingredients of No Oven Simple Tea cake

  1. It's 2 of large eggs at room temperature.
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp of milk at room temperature.
  3. You need 1 1/2 tsp of pure vanilla extract.
  4. It's 1 1/4 cups of flour.
  5. It's 1 tsp of baking powder.
  6. You need 1/4 tsp of salt.
  7. You need 3/4 cup of powdered white sugar.
  8. You need 3/4 cup of unsalted butter at room temperature.

No Oven Simple Tea cake instructions

  1. Beat butter and powder sugar ;.
  2. Add eggs one by one ; beat well until fluffy.
  3. Add vanilla extract; salt and milk and beat well.
  4. In separate bowl ; add flour, baking powder and mix well.
  5. Add dry ingredients in egg mixture....
  6. Pour in greased mold.
  7. Bake in it in pre heated pan approx for 35 mins.
  8. Dish out and enjoy with tea.
