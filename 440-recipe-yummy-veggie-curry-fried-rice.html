Veggie Curry Fried Rice step by step

  1. Heat Olive Oil in a Large Non-stick Skillet over Medium Heat.
  2. Add Garlic, Onion and saut√© for 2 minutes,.
  3. Add Potatoes and cook until they turn golden and crusty. It takes about 4 to 5 minutes.
  4. Add curry powder, turmeric powder and stir them well.
  5. Add tomato to the skillet and keep cooking for 5 minutes.
  6. Add Carrots and simmer until most tomato sauce has evaporated.
  7. Add rice and mix them well.
  8. Add salt and pepper.
  9. Ready to serve.
