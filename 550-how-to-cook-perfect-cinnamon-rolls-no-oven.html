Cinnamon rolls no oven instructions

  1. Mix flour sugar baking powder and salt together in a bowl..
  2. Add butter into the mixture and rub using your hands.
  3. Beat milk and egg together in another bowl, pour into the flour mixture mix till soft dough forms.
  4. Turn dough into floured surface and roll into quarter inch thick rectangle. Brush surface with melted butter.
  5. Mix sugar and cinnamon together in a small bowl and sprinkle over the dough..
