Ingredients of Nasi Goreng Mawut (Mixed Fried Rice and Noodles)

  1. It's 2 of plates cooked white rice (leftover rice, put in the refrigerator for a night, is recommended).
  2. You need 100 gr of yellow noodles, soaked in warm water for 5-10 minutes, drained.
  3. It's 1 bunch of mustard greens, cut 3 cm.
  4. Prepare 150 gr of boneless chicken breast, boiled, shredded, set aside.
  5. It's 3 Tbsp of sweet soy sauce.
  6. It's to taste of salt and mushrooms bouillon.
  7. Prepare 2 Tbsp of vegetable oil.
  8. You need of Ground spices:.
  9. Prepare 3 cloves of garlic.
  10. Prepare 2 pcs of candle nuts 4 chilli peppers.
  11. You need 2 of bird eye chilli peppers (optional).
  12. Prepare 1/4 tsp of white peppercorn.
  13. Prepare of Complements.
  14. Prepare 2 of sunny side up eggs.
  15. You need of some rice crackers.

Nasi Goreng Mawut (Mixed Fried Rice and Noodles) instructions

  1. Heat vegetable oil in the wok over medium-heat. Sauté the ground spices until fragrant. Add shredded chicken. Mix well..
  2. Add mustard greens. Cook until the vegetables a bit soft..
  3. Add rice and yellow noodles. Season with sweet soy sauce, salt, and mushroom bouillon. Mix well. Remove from the heat..
  4. Place fried rice on the serving plates. Add sunny side up eggs and rice crackers on top of the rice. Serve warm. Yum! 😋.
