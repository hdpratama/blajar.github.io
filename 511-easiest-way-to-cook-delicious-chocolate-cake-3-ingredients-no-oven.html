Ingredients of Chocolate cake (3 ingredients, no oven)

  1. You need 28 of Oreo cookies.
  2. You need 1 Cup of milk (245ml).
  3. Prepare 1 Tsp of Baking Powder.

Chocolate cake (3 ingredients, no oven) step by step

  1. Separate the Oreo cookie crust from the cream into two bowls..
  2. Place the crust into a ziplock plastic bag and smash it until itâ€™s dust. You can use a blender for this or do it yourself..
  3. Once you are done abusing the cookies, put it in a bowl and add 1 Tsp of Baking Powder..
  4. Mix, mix, mix..
  5. Pour in a cup of milk (about 245ml) and add the cream from the cookies you separated earlier..
  6. Mix again until the cream and powder are dissolved..
  7. Use a pan and smother cooking oil on it (for non-stick purposes) and then put in baking paper..
  8. Pour the Oreo mixture into the pan..
  9. Turn on the stove and place a wider pan on it to even out the temperature..
  10. Next put the Oreo mixture pan on top of the other pan. Remember to put a lid on the pan..
  11. Now put it aside on low for 40-50 minutes..
  12. Now take it out of the oven..
  13. For chocolate glaze, pour in 150ml of hot whipping cream into a bowl of dark chocolate. Mix it until it dissolves and when it cools pour it on top of the cake..
  14. And finally to decorate, you can use grated chocolate or fruits. Or if you have some extra Oreos you can use that!.
  15. Enjoy the cake!.
