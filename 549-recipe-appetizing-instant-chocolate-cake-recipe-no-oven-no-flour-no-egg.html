Ingredients of Instant Chocolate Cake Recipe| No Oven | No Flour | No Egg

  1. You need 1/2 cup of Cream Cheese.
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp of Sugar (Extra if you want).
  3. You need 200 ml of Whipping Cream.
  4. You need 1 tsp of Vanilla Extract.
  5. Prepare 2oo ml of Chocolate Milk.
  6. You need 4 of Sweet Bread Slices.
  7. You need As required of Chocolate Shavings.
  8. You need As needed of Cherries.

Instant Chocolate Cake Recipe| No Oven | No Flour | No Egg step by step

  1. Take bread slices and cut edges..
  2. In a bowl add cream cheese and sugar, whisk well..
  3. Add whipping cream, vanilla extract and whisk well until stiff..
  4. In a bowl, add chocolate milk and soak the bread for a few seconds and keep that aside. (Do not soak the bread too long)..
  5. Take a cake board and spread 2 tbsp of whipped cream, place one slice of bread and spread whipped cream on it evenly..
  6. Continue to stack the bread and whipped cream..
  7. Once you finish with stacking the fourth bread slice, then cover the cake with the whipped cream..
  8. Sprinkle with some chocolate shavings and place some cherries..
